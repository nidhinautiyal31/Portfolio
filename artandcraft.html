Art and Craft

Art and craft are two distinct yet interconnected forms of creative expression. In art, personal creativity is paramount as viewers' interpretations often challenge traditional boundaries. Craft, on the other hand, values skill and precision in creating functional or decorative objects that adhere to established traditions and techniques. Despite their differences, both art and craft contribute to human creativity and cultural diversity while blurring the line between them at times.
